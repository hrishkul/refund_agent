1. SCOPE AND PURPOSE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy governs all refund, return, exchange, and cancellation
requests processed through the ShopEase platform. It applies to all
customers across Standard and Premium tiers. Any exceptions require
written approval from a Senior Operations Manager.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. CORE REFUND RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Rule 1 — Final Sale Items
Items explicitly marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase are
non-refundable and non-exchangeable under any circumstances, including
customer remorse, sizing issues, or change of preference. This
restriction cannot be waived by any support agent or system.

Rule 2 — Return Window
Refund requests must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the
original purchase date. Customers enrolled in the ShopEase Premium
Membership program are entitled to an extended window of 45 calendar
days. Requests received after the applicable window will be
automatically denied without exception.

Rule 3 — High-Value Escalation Threshold
All refund requests where the order total exceeds $500.00 USD must be
escalated to a human Senior Support Agent for manual review and
approval. Automated systems may not approve or deny these requests.
The escalation target response time is 24 business hours.

Rule 4 — Defective or Damaged Merchandise
Items confirmed as defective, damaged in transit, or materially
mis-described are eligible for a full refund or replacement at the
customer's choice, provided the claim is raised within 60 calendar
days of delivery. Supporting evidence (photo or video) may be
requested by the support agent.

Rule 5 — Digital Products
Digital goods (software, templates, e-books, downloadable content)
are non-refundable once the download has been initiated or the
license key has been redeemed. If a download has not been initiated,
a full refund may be issued within 7 days of purchase.

Rule 6 — In-Transit Orders and Cancellations
Orders that have been shipped but not yet delivered cannot be
cancelled or immediately refunded. The customer must wait for
delivery and then initiate a standard return. Orders in "pending" or
"processing" status may be cancelled for a full refund at no penalty.

3. EXTENDED POLICY RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Rule 7 — Gift Orders
Orders placed as gifts (where the purchaser and recipient differ) are
eligible for store credit only, not cash or card refunds, unless the
item is confirmed defective under Rule 4. Gift refunds cannot exceed
the original purchase price. The gift recipient may initiate the
return; the store credit will be issued to the original purchaser's
account. The standard return window (Rule 2) applies.

Rule 8 — Holiday Extended Return Window
Orders placed between November 15 and December 31 of any calendar
year qualify for an extended return deadline of January 31 of the
following year, regardless of Standard or Premium membership status.
This extension applies only to the return window; all other rules
(Final Sale, Digital, High-Value Escalation) remain in full effect.
The holiday window cannot be combined with the Premium 45-day window;
whichever deadline is later applies.

Rule 9 — Partial Refunds and Quantity Discrepancies
Where an order contains multiple units of the same item and only a
subset is being returned, a partial refund proportional to the
returned quantity will be issued. The per-unit refund amount is
calculated as: (original order total) / (quantity ordered) * (quantity
returned). Partial refunds on orders exceeding $500 total still
require human escalation per Rule 3. Partial refunds on Final Sale
items are not permitted per Rule 1.

4. REFUND PROCESSING TIMELINES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within
5–7 business days. Store credit is applied instantly. Escalated cases
targeting human review are resolved within 24 business hours.
ShopEase is not liable for additional processing delays imposed by
the customer's bank or card network.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. DISPUTES AND CHARGEBACKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Customers who initiate a chargeback with their payment provider while
a ShopEase refund request is under review will have their active
request automatically closed. ShopEase will contest chargebacks
where the refund was denied in accordance with this policy.

6. POLICY ENFORCEMENT AND AMENDMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy is enforced automatically by the ShopEase AI support
system. No support agent, automated or human, may approve a refund
that violates Rules 1, 5, or 6. Rules 3, 7, 8, and 9 may be
overridden only by a Senior Operations Manager with written
justification. ShopEase reserves the right to amend this policy with
30 days' notice published on the platform.
